About this Site

Welcome to the Writer's Toolkit! This site was made as a place for writers to easily access helpful resources which may benefit their writing. These tools were made to help both beginner and intermediate authors alike, and help them get into the world of writing. This website was made by Lauren Valencia, an aspiring scientist who writes as a passionate hobby for her Computer Science 3 class.
