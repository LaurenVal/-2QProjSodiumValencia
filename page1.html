Writer's Toolkit

Character Outline

Questions to ask:

What is their name?

What is their age (number or age group)?

What pronouns do they use?

What is their sexual or romantic orientation? (optional, but reccommended)

What is their physical appearance?

What are some defining personality traits?

What is their most fatal character flaw?

How do they view the world?

What would make them break?

Who would they get along best with?

Example

Sephone

24 (young adult)

She/her

Lesbian



Short greyish-brown hair, shady green eyes, pale skin.

Her sarcasm, realism, and gratitude.

Her reliance on others.

Imperfect, yet still good enough.

Losing someone close to her.

Someone who is positive and kind to her no matter what.
